ABOUT US

Entrepreneurship Cell NIT-Bhopal is a non-profit student organization that works to support and unleash the budding entrepreneurial minds and promote the start-up culture among the students. We at E-Cell link students with mentors, seekers with investors and aspiring entrepreneurs with enduring entrepreneurs. By organizing several entrepreneurship development programmes and camps, skill development workshops and speaker sessions, hosting brainstorming competitions, we aim to promote the development of knowledge-based enterprises and open doors towards opportunities in inventive areas. Connected with a prominent alumni network and established entrepreneurs,E-Cell NIT-B thrives to serve the purpose of providing necessary resources and potential guidance to spirited individuals.
